🚀 HOW IT WORKS (User Flow)
────────────────────────────

1. User arrives at journal page
   ↓
2. Selects "🎤 Voice" mode (or stays with "✏️ Type")
   ↓
3. Taps "🎤 Start Recording"
   ↓
4. Speaks journal entry naturally
   ↓
5. Watches waveform animate and sees live transcription
   ↓
6. Taps "⏹️ Stop" when done speaking
   ↓
7. Reviews and confirms transcribed text
   ↓
8. Taps "Send to Solace ♥"
   ↓
9. AI generates warm, personalized response
   ↓
10. User taps "🔊 Play Response" to hear Solace's voice
    ↓
11. Entry saved with mood, text, and input method (voice/text)
