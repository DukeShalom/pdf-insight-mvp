# 🎤 Solace Voice Features — Quick Start Guide

## What's New?

Solace now lets users journal using their **voice** — no typing required. You can also **hear** Solace's responses read aloud in a warm, caring voice.

---

## 🚀 How to Use Voice

### Step 1: Switch to Voice Mode
On the journal page, tap the **"🎤 Voice"** button (next to the "✏️ Type" button).

### Step 2: Start Recording
1. Tap the **"🎤 Start Recording"** button
2. You'll see a **red pulsing waveform** — this means Solace is listening
3. **Speak naturally** about how you're feeling
4. Your words appear in real-time as you speak

### Step 3: Stop Recording
Tap the **"⏹️ Stop"** button when you're done speaking.

Your complete transcription appears below the recording buttons.

### Step 4: Submit Your Entry
Tap **"Send to Solace ♥"** just like normal.

### Step 5: Hear Solace's Response
After Solace responds, a **"🔊 Play Response"** button appears.

Tap it to hear Solace's warm response read aloud.

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

1. **Speak clearly** — Don't mumble or rush your words
2. **Use natural pauses** — Solace recognizes where sentences end
3. **Minimize background noise** — Quiet environments work best
4. **Be authentic** — Speak like you're talking to a friend
5. **Edit if needed** — Your transcription can be edited before submitting

---

## 🔄 Switching Between Modes

You can **switch between text and voice** anytime:
- Tap **"✏️ Type"** to go back to typing
- Tap **"🎤 Voice"** to switch to voice recording
- Your previous text/recording is preserved when you switch back

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

### Microphone Permission Denied?
- Check your browser settings (⚙️ → Site settings → Microphone)
- Reload the page after granting permission

### Speech Not Being Recognized?
- Speak louder and more clearly
- Reduce background noise
- Try a different browser

### Can't Hear Solace's Response?
- Check your device volume
- Verify browser speaker permissions
- Try refreshing the page

### Is My Voice Private?
- **Yes!** Everything happens in your browser
- No audio is sent to servers (MVP version)
- Your data stays completely private
